Joint Learning For Prosodic Break Presiction and Mel Spectrogram Generation in Chinese Text to Speech

Abstract

    Although the end-to-end speech synthesis method, such as Tacotron2, can generate fluently and natural English speech. But there is still suffered from some prosody problem in Chinese speech synthesis, such as the phrase break errors occur frequently when synthesizing long sentences. In this paper, to solve the problem in the mandarin TTS, we propose the HP-Tacotron2, a multi-task framework including the hierarchical prosodic break predictor and Mel-spectrogram generator. We treat the prosody break label as the additional conditioning feature and concatenate it with the encoder’s output to a joint vector for the Mel-spectrogram generation task. By multi-task training strategy, not only improves the prediction performance of the prosody model be improved but also makes the synthesized audio can be made more natural. And further, improve the quality of the multi-task framework, we introduce more efficient syntactic features by syntactic parsing. We evaluate different systems on objective and subjective experiments and the results of the experiment show that the proposed ap-proach outperforms the other systems.
